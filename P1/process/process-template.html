P1:Storybook Process

Inspiration

For the response to the question, two topics came to mind:
1.A friend's cat's birthday party
2.Christmas parcel sent by my mom
After contemplating between the two, I decided to create a small web storybook inspired by the Christmas parcel sent by my mom.

Initial sketches and concepts

Using the Figma program, I began sketching. Initially, I aimed to capture the year-end atmosphere by combining Christmas colors for the theme. I conceptualized the sketch by focusing on the concept of opening the box and discovering what was inside. However, it proved to be more challenging than expected, and the colors were too dark, failing to convey the happiness and bright feelings associated with receiving a parcel. Therefore, I decided to make modifications and changed the color theme to brighter tones.

Refined concepts and in-process work

I simplified the objects as much as possible by overlapping or sticking basic shapes together. This eventually led to a lot of iterations and, in the end, resulted in a final design somewhat different from the initial concept.

Final work

The process involved refining the design to incorporate brighter colors and maintain a sense of simplicity. The final product deviated slightly from the initial design seen in the process work but captured the essence of the joy associated with receiving a parcel.
