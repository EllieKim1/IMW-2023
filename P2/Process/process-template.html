P2:Poster Process

Inspiration

I found the Visiting Designer Lecture Series to be the most captivating content package, so I started my research. Here's what I learned from my research: I've decided to use the colors blue, white, and black.

Initial sketches and concepts

Using the Figma program, I established the initial concept and received feedback. I attempted to change the colors and received feedback about redundant words and too much text on the first screen. Consequently, I decided to make revisions accordingly.

Refined concepts and in-process work

Due to time constraints, I focused on fixing other aspects and started coding based on the prototype created in Figma. While keeping simplicity in mind, the HTML was straightforward, but animating proved to be challenging. In particular, changing the mouse cursor to an image was more difficult than anticipated. Despite inputting the image in CSS, it didn't change at all, indicating an issue I still need to address.

Final work

I spent a lot of time implementing the animations I planned in CSS rather than resolving the cursor issue, and it turned out well as I had envisioned. However, I still need to work on changing the appearance of the cursor. The animations were inspired by the artworks of the artists and interactive elements on websites. Hovering over the artist's name displays their description.
